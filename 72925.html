Transforming Africa Through Entrepreneurship: Strive Masiyiwa

Thursday, November 30, 2017

12:10 pm

CEMEX Auditorium

Sponsored by:
Africa Business Club and the Center for Entrepreneurial Studies, Stanford Graduate School of Business

  • Share
  • Tweet
  • Add to calendar
  • Email

Strive Masiyiwa is the founder and executive chairman of Econet, a global telecommunications group in more than 15 countries. He is also chairman of the Alliance for a Green Revolution in Africa and chair of the Micronutrient Initiative, focused on ending child hunger and improving nutrition. Strive was selected to Fortune's 2014 "World's 50 Greatest Leaders," and in 2015 was awarded the International Rescue Committee's Freedom award and the UN Foundation Global Leadership award for the Africa Against Ebola Solidarity Trust.

When:
Thursday, November 30, 2017
12:10 pm – 1:10 pm
Where:
CEMEX Auditorium
Admission:

This event is free and open to the public.
